About Us

-Dr. Bhanu Pratap Chander

Dr Bhanu Pratap is the founder of Samhitha ENT & Child Care Centre. He has done his MBBS from Mamatha Medical College & MS (ENT) from Gandhi Medical College, Hyderabad. He did Fellowship of Advanced Sinus and Endoscopic Anterior Skullbase Surgery under the Great Dr Narayanan Janakiram , Royal Pearl Hospital, Tamilnadu and Observership in Obstructive Sleep Apnea - Snoring surgeries under Dr Vikas Agrawal , Specialty ENT hospital Mumbai.

Samhitha ENT & Child Care Centre, founded in 2021 under the leadership of Dr. Bhanu Pratap Chander, has evolved from a humble beginning to one of the best ENT specialty hospital in Hyderabad. Samhitha ENT proudly offers a range of multidisciplinary healthcare options. The team at Samhitha includes specialists in Rhinoplasty, Tympanoplasty, Balloon Sinuplasty, Pediatrics, and more. This integrated approach ensures you receive holistic healthcare all under one roof, providing convenience and continuity in your medical journey.His moto and philosophy is to deliver the best of ENT care to his dear patients

About Us

-Dr. Sonika Bashetty

"Hand-holding through the process of recovery, creating a bond of trust, assurance and reliability"

Dr Sonika is the director of Samhitha ENT & Child Care Centre. She did her MBBS from Osmania Medical College, DCH from Gandhi Medical College and finished her DNB from Little stars Childrens hospital under the eminent Pediatricians Dr Satish Ghanta , Dr Mehul Shah , Renowned Pediatric Nephrologist and Dr Ramana Dandamudi , Renowned Pediatric Hemato- oncologist. She was the University topper in the DCh examination conducted by Dr NTR University of Health sciences.

Samhitha aims to offer individualized, round-the-clock advanced care in a medically sophisticated, yet intensely child-centered environment. Samhitha's cadre of trained paediatricians, experienced nursing staff, therapists and other supporting clinicians work together to provide both intensive and innovative multidisciplinary clinical and therapeutic care. Samhitha Peditric Centre hope to help children achieve their medical and rehabilitative goals, and return to their communities as the bright future of our country.
